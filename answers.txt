Q3: What is the difference between merging and rebasing?

Answer: Git merge combines the history of both branches that you are merging 
and merges them onto a single branch. Git rebase rewrites the history of the 
new branch by ordering the changes of both branches together, making the 
rebased branch's history more organized and linear.
