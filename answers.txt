Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give and Example and how it might help

Answer: Data science can be applied to the healthcare industry when tracking 
diseases and pandemics. We can easily monitor and track the rate that diseases 
will travel by using the vast amounts of data that we can gather. Data science 
can apply to basically any health care application that utilizes a lot of 
data. Many of these applications are in regards to diseases and medicines 
that are tracked based on patient data.
